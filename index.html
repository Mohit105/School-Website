Achivements

Football

In the school, Teams are made by all the kids who want to participate (around 100 kids) and there are teams all with ten kids each team. Then, the tournament begins. All the teams go until the one team is the winner of the tournament it usually lasts around three weeks. Then the tournament ends with the top 2 teams playing against each other and whoever has the most goals at the end of the match, wins. Then the teams are made again the next school year.

Debate club

Every year, when the school starts, the debate club starts and prepares to hosts a school-wide debate on a topic chosen yearly. This year, the topic was climate change specifically what type of pollution contributes the most to climate change. The members of the club were divided into 2 teams each with their own specific topic on pollution; water pollution and air pollution. Each team had to research on their topic and at the time of the main debate, the 2 teams go against each other. The team with the strongest points win the debate.

Science Fair

The school science fair is an amazing time for all creative and innovative students to showcase their talents. Every year, the school hosts a school-wide science fair where students can pick a topic of their liking and create a model, invention, idea board, or project they create for the topic. They then present what they have made to the school judges who are the Principal, the vice-principal and the head of the science departmeant. The top 3 creations and given first, second, and third prize awards.
